**Eyebrow** — a small, letter-spaced uppercase label. The brand's quiet caps voice; sits above a heading or marks a section (as in the brand sheet's "PERSONAL BRAND GUIDE").

```jsx
<Eyebrow>Personal Brand Guide</Eyebrow>
<h2>Selected writing</h2>
```

Defaults to Slate Teal; pass `color="var(--brass-600)"` for the rare brass marker. 12px, +0.16em tracking. Keep it short — two or three words.
