**Pullquote** — a quote or motto in Fraunces italic, the brand's classical voice. Use for the motto, testimonials, or a featured line.

```jsx
<Pullquote cite="Brand motto">
  Be true · act freely · understand deeply
</Pullquote>
```

`align="left"` (default) carries a 2px brass tick on the left; `align="center"` drops it for formal, centred use. Keep the measure short — depth reveals itself, it doesn't shout.
